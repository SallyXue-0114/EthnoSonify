About EthnoSonify

EthnoSonify is the senior capstone project of Sally Xue (CS+Music, UIUC). It visualizes population changes among representative ethnic groups across 8 provinces in China between 2000 and 2020, using a combination of color-coded geographic maps and narrative storytelling.

In addition to visual data, EthnoSonify explores data sonification by sampling traditional instruments from various ethnic groups. The population increase is represented through the layering and richness of harmony and instrumentation.

To enhance understanding, the project includes curated images and video clips of ethnic instruments, allowing users to engage more intuitively with the cultural context behind the data.
